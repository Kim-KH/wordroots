This app does not collect or transmit any personal information to external servers.

1. Information We Collect

Word Roots does not collect any personally identifiable information.

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your selected display language (Korean, English, Japanese, Chinese, or French)
  • Memo notes you write for each word group

This data remains on your device and is never transmitted to any server or external system.

2. Internet Access

All core features (vocabulary study, memos, language switching) work fully offline. The text-to-speech pronunciation feature uses your device's built-in TTS engine and does not communicate with any external server.

3. Third-Party Sharing

Since no personal data is collected, there is nothing to share with third parties. The app contains no advertisements, analytics tools, or tracking SDKs.

4. Children's Privacy

This app is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. No personal information is collected from any user.

5. Data Deletion

All locally stored memos and settings are completely removed when you uninstall the app.
